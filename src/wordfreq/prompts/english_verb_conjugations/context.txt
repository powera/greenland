English verbs conjugate across 3 tenses and 6 persons:

**Tenses:**
1. Present tense
2. Past tense
3. Future tense

**Persons:**
1. 1st person singular (I)
2. 2nd person singular (you)
3. 3rd person singular (he/she/it)
4. 1st person plural (we)
5. 2nd person plural (you)
6. 3rd person plural (they)

**Imperative:**
1. 2nd person singular imperative (you, singular - command form)
2. 2nd person plural imperative (you, plural - command form)

**Important notes:**
- Most English verbs only inflect in 3rd person singular present (e.g., "he walks")
- Past tense is typically the same for all persons (e.g., "I walked", "they walked")
- Future tense uses "will" + infinitive (e.g., "I will walk", "they will walk")
- Imperative is the bare infinitive (e.g., "walk!")
