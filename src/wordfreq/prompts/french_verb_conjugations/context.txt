French verbs conjugate across 6 persons and 6 tenses:

**Persons:**
1. 1st person singular (je - I)
2. 2nd person singular (tu - you, informal)
3. 3rd person singular (il/elle/on - he/she/one)
4. 1st person plural (nous - we)
5. 2nd person plural (vous - you, formal/plural)
6. 3rd person plural (ils/elles - they)

**Tenses:**
1. Present (présent de l'indicatif) - je mange
2. Imperfect (imparfait) - je mangeais
3. Future (futur simple) - je mangerai
4. Conditional (conditionnel présent) - je mangerais
5. Subjunctive present (subjonctif présent) - que je mange
6. Passé composé (compound past) - j'ai mangé

**Important notes:**
- Include the pronoun with each form (e.g., "je mange" not just "mange")
- For irregular verbs (être, avoir, aller, faire, etc.), provide the correct irregular forms
- Regular -er, -ir, -re verbs follow standard patterns
- Passé composé uses auxiliary (avoir or être) + past participle
- Some verbs use être as auxiliary (aller → je suis allé, venir → je suis venu)
- Pay attention to agreement with subject for être verbs
- Use empty string ("") for any forms that are non-standard or don't exist
