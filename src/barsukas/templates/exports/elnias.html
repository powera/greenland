Bootstrap Format:
The export includes minimal data for bootstrapping:
  • English word
  • Target language translation
  • GUID (word identifier)

  • POS (part of speech) - excludes verbs
  • Subtype (categorization)
Note: Verbs are excluded from this export due to wild differences in conjugation types between languages.
