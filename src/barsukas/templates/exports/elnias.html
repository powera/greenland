Bootstrap Format:
The export includes minimal data for bootstrapping:
  • English word
  • Target language translation
  • GUID (word identifier)
  • Trakaido level (difficulty) - might be removable in future
  • POS (part of speech) - includes all parts of speech, including verbs
  • Subtype (categorization)
Note: Only base forms are exported (no conjugations or grammatical forms). For detailed verb conjugations, use the WireWord export instead.
